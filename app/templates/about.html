What we do

Every year, Italian supermarkets waste around 220 000 tons of food.

The main goal of RunOut is to reduce food waste, connecting food-sellers and potential customers in order to highlight the discounted near-expiry products still available in the stores.

Benefits for customers

Customers can easily find discounted food in nearby supermarkets, being able to decrease the cost of groceries. They can also create a handy shopping list to help them keep track of the products during the actual shopping in the various stores. It is also possible to write and read reviews about the different supermarkets, giving the possibility to share advice and opinions among the customers.

Benefits for food-sellers

The waste of food is not only an environmental problem but also a waste of resources and money for the whole industry. Our 3 main commercial partners (Coop, Conad and Esselunga) are now able to promote their near expiring product on a dedicated platform. They can apply a discount to an item if its due date is nearing, increasing the possibility of selling it and avoiding waste and loss of money. The platform is also a new and free way to advertise their store locations in major cities to new customers.

Conad is an Italian retail store brand which operates one of the largest supermarket chains in Italy. Created in 1962, it is a cooperative system of entrepreneurs, dealing in large-scale distribution.

   

 

 

1444 stores in Italy

Coop is a system of Italian consumers' cooperatives which operates the largest supermarket chain in Italy. Its headquarters are located in Casalecchio di Reno, Province of Bologna. The first cooperative shop was established in Turin in 1854.

   

 

 

160 stores in Italy

Esselunga S.p.A. is an Italian retail store chain founded in 1957. It was the first supermarket chain in Italy to introduce on-line shopping and self-produced organic products.

       

Who are we?

We are a start-up made of 6 students from the Polytechnic of Turin. We have different engineering background spacing from mechanical, management, aerospace and biomedical and now studying for an MSC in Engineering and Management. Our group can also count on a strong multicultural composition having members both from Europe and the Middle East. We have always been very passionate about the environmental problems still present in our day-to-day lives; therefore, we decide to use our skills to deal with the waste of food that everyday occurs in the supermarkets industry.
